Top 3 Potato Shapes

Partner pg External site
  1. French Fry Style

    A classic! Don't fix something that is not broken, right? Easiest shape fit into the small ketchup cups too. Also very managable to eat while on the road. A standard beside every burger, great with wings too. Can be dipped in various sauces.

  2. Waffle Fries

    My personal favorite. Has much more of a warm-soft center, surface area wise that everyone loves! Can hold many more toppings making it more of an appetizer than a side. This option retains heat much better, don't ask me about the science behind that though.

  3. Tator-Tots

    The cruchiest of them all. Can be hot to the touch so be careful! I would highly recommend using this as the base for a yummy breakfast bowl. This option has to be the most fun to play with, although you really should not be playing with your food!
